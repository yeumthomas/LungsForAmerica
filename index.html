4 Steps to Save a Life

Our movement wants to use the power of the increased amount of tagging and sharing on social media that has begun ever since US-wide quarantines were mandated. We want you to take a breath, take a short break from whatever you are doing, and pledge to do some push-ups! Maybe even donate a dollar or two! Our goal is to fund ApolloBVM ventilators engineered by Rice University students, which are only 1% the cost of the conventional ventilator which is $20,000 - 30,000. Together, we will conquer COVID-19.
1. Post the template (click here) on your instagram story and tag 3 friends!
2. When someone wants to donate, they will venmo you and you can cross that $ amount off your bingo board and repost!
3. When you complete a board, visit @lungsforamerica to donate!
4. Remember to do 1 push-up per $1 donated!
