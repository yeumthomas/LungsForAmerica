5 Steps to Save a Life

Our movement wants to take advantage of the increased amount of tagging and sharing on social media that has begun ever since US-wide quarantines were mandated. We want you to take a breath, take a short break from whatever you are doing, and pledge to do some push-ups! Maybe even donate a dollar or two! Our goal is to fund ApolloBVM ventilators engineered by Rice University students, which are only 1% the cost of the conventional ventilator which is $20,000 - 30,000. Together, we will conquer COVID-19.
1. Post the template (click here) on your instagram story and tag 3 friends!
2. When someone wants to donate, they will visit ig: @lungsforamerica to pledge!
3. In the donation form, the donator should venmo us and state the name of the pledger they are donating for.
4. You will be emailed about who donated on your behalf and can crop a picture of their face on the lung with the amount they donated and repost!
5. Once you fill the board, do 1 pushup per $1 donated!
