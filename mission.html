Why are we here?

Mission Statement: Bringing low-cost ventilators to hospitals in need through social media campaigns. But we can't do it without you.

Our Vision

By using social media, we can hit the ground running, and create a cascading effect. By allowing each pledger to tag 3 people on their Instagram stories to join our campaign, we will be able to increase the amount of money donated, and thus the number of ventilators produced and shipped exponentially.
This is a conservative projection, as for each donor, their posts will reach anywhere from 200-2000 people and a total of 7 people will be directly tagged to these post (3 via sharing and 4 who are donors). With this prediction, we can provie 616 ventilators in only 21 days! This goes to show how impactful our movement can be.
